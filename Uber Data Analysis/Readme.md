## Retail Sales and Profit Analysis Dashboard

Overview:  
Cleaned and analyzed a uber data analysis dataset from [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhanupratapbiswas/uber-data-analysis/data). 
Python library pandas is used for cleaning the dataset.Also Exploratory Data Analysis is done. Additional column ROUTE is added to show vizualise frequency of routes.
KPIs are calulated in Tableau. Also interactive dashboard is created.

## Insights
- Uber usage is heavily business dominated with 93.33%.
- 43.46% of rides are categorized under 'Unknown' purpose. Among known purposes, Meetings are the top driver of rides.
- Rides are lowest in early morning hours.Peak time of the day is 2 PM- 6PM.
- Pickup locations show uneven distribution. Cary has the highest ride counts 201. Many locations have lower ride counts.
- Route frequency varies widely. The most frequent route is unknown location to unknown location.Other commonly occuring routes include Morrisville-Cary, Cary-Cary, and Cary-Durham.
